📲 Install on iPhone & use offline
GPS needs no phone signal. The satellites broadcast straight to your phone, and every line of this app is stored on the device — nothing is downloaded while you sail.

Install it:
1 · Open this file in Safari (not the grey Files preview).
2 · Tap Share (the square with an ↑).
3 · Choose Add to Home Screen, then Add.
4 · Launch from the new Passage icon — it opens full-screen with no Safari bars.

Prove it offline at the dock: turn on Aeroplane Mode (GPS still works on iPhone), open the app, tap START and check it shows your position and a speed. If it works in Aeroplane Mode on the pontoon, it works in the Celtic Sea.

Underway: keep "keep screen awake" on, keep the app in front (iOS pauses GPS when the screen sleeps or you switch apps), and keep the phone on charge.
